APJ Abdul Kalam's Quotes For Students

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, also known by his initials A.P.J., was a scientist and statesman from India who rendered significant contributions to the advancement of India's space and missile projects.

He is especially prominent for serving at the helm during India's nuclear weapons experiments in 1998, establishing the country as a nuclear power. Kalam consistently emphasized the positive applications of technology and the significance of harnessing science and technology for societal advancement, despite his engagement in both the Defence and Technology industries. Due to his valiant efforts, Kalam is affectionately known as the "Missile Man of India."
